On Your Special Day

Remember how loved you are by all those around you. Enjoy every moment!

A close friend is one
Of the most important
Things in life, and if
You’re lucky enough
To find that person,
You’ll have found
A companion for life.

I am lucky that I found
That in you, my dear.

Happy birthday to my Companion in life: my All-time bestie!
